Chicken Parmesan

Description

The recipe takes delicate ingredients sourced from around the world idealy and combines them into a masterfull cooking and eating experience for all involved. This is made possible only through the immense levels of culinary craftsmanship developed over the thousands of years of chicken parmesan creation on earth.

In order to best serve this dish, remember to wash all ingredients thoroughly and commandeer the room as you prepare the dish because spectacle is a chef's best friend.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Moldovan Garden Leaf
  • Scarlet Sauce
  • Log of Mozarella
  • Truffle Oil from The Swiss Alps

Steps

  1. Take the chicken thighs and dip them into the parmesan cheese.
  2. Deep fry in oil
  3. Place in baking sheet and add scarlet sauce over the top
  4. Place mozerarella slices as well over the chicken before placing in 500 degree oven
  5. Finally, garnish with the garden leaf and you are ready to serve
