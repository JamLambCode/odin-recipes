Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is an American classic. And so, knowing how to make one is extremely important when attempting to cultivate a realistic national identity.

But put your chauvenism aside, this is what you will need to make the tried and true recipe...

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • White Bread

Steps

  1. Put peanut butter on slice of bread
  2. Put jelly on other slice of bread
  3. Join two slices of bread together with the ingredients facing eachother
